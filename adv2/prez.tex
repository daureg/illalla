\begin{frame}{Even more statistics}

Distribution of the distance from tags to their center of gravity
$G_t =   (\bar{x_i}, \bar{y_i})$:
$\mathcal{D}(t) = \{||p_i - G_t||^2\}_{i=1, \dots, T}$

where $R$ is one of the $g^4$ possible rectangular region.
